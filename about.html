ABOUT JOVEN
The Joven Story

A brand of Innovation striving for comfort in every home since 1983

JOVEN Electric Co Sdn. Bhd., the brainchild of our founder Mr. James V.G. Tan was established on 12 Oct 1983. The idea came about with the intention to produce more affordable water heaters, so that we do not have to depend on more expensive imported water heaters. JOVEN was the 1st Malaysian Electric Water Heater Manufacturer in Malaysia.

As a small start-up in 1983, JOVEN began producing its very first water heater from its factory in Kiang, Malaysia, and we have now expanded into a 118,000 sq. ft. manufacturing plant and exporting to 11 countries with a workforce of about 120 workers.

Innovation at its very core, JOVEN always believes in safety and reliability in all of its products. Joven brings innovation home with the introduction of home appliances products, from Instant Water Heaters, Storage Water Heaters, Multipoint Water Heaters, Water Purifiers, Centralized Automatic Domestic Water Pumps to DC ceiling fans.

JOVEN has been recognized as the No.1 Malaysian Brand in Water Heater by IPSOS research in 2013. Celebrating 35 years of product and brand excellence, JOVEN will continue to propelto the forefront and grow in its export markets to achieve recognition as a truly global brand.
